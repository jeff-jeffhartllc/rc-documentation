# Getting help

## Common issues

| Issue | What to try |
| --- | --- |
| Cannot sign in to Domo | Contact your Domo administrator; confirm your account on regiscorp.domo.com |
| Cannot open REGIS FRANCHISEE APP | Confirm you were granted access to this app |
| Blank or stale cards | Contact your administrator — data may still be refreshing |
| A salon is missing from filters | Contact your administrator to confirm your salon assignments |
| Export not available | Confirm your Domo role allows export, or ask an administrator |

## Who to contact

Reach your Regis Domo administrator or franchisee support contact for access changes, missing data, or report questions.

# About this guide

**Audience:** Corporate daily users and analysts  
**App:** REGIS APP on https://regiscorp.domo.com

This guide covers **day-to-day use** of **REGIS APP** — navigation, filters, pages, exports, and sharing.

Franchisee operators use a separate file: **Regis-Franchisee-Guide.docx**. Do not distribute this corporate User Guide to franchisee end users.

If you cannot access the app, see missing data, or need a change to cards or reports, contact your Regis Domo administrator. Escalation contacts are listed in the Admin Guide.

## Purpose

The **Daily Sales Email Report** page provides a daily sales summary suitable for email distribution to stakeholders. This page is **corporate-only** — it does not appear in REGIS FRANCHISEE APP.

## Opening the page

1. REGIS APP → **Daily Sales Email Report**  
   https://regiscorp.domo.com/app-studio/183500481/pages/1756569987

## Common tasks

### Task: Distribute the daily email report

1. Open the page and apply desired filters.
2. Click **Distribute** in the page toolbar.
3. Configure recipients, schedule, and format.
4. **Expected result:** Report is sent on schedule to configured recipients.

See [Exports and sharing](./exports-and-sharing.md) for detailed distribution steps.

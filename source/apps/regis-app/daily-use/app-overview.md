## Relationship to REGIS FRANCHISEE APP

REGIS FRANCHISEE APP is a **subset** of this application, intended for franchisee users. It reuses content and data from REGIS APP but applies **Personalized Data Permissions (PDP)** so each franchisee sees only their own stores.

When maintaining REGIS APP, consider downstream impact on the franchisee app. Changes to shared pages, cards, datasets, or PDP rules may affect franchisee visibility.

See `shared/regis-app-relationship.pdf` for the full cross-app reference.

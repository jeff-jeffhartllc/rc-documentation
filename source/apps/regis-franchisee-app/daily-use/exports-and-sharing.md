## Purpose

How to export data from REGIS FRANCHISEE APP. Exports reflect the salons available on your account.

## Export a card

1. Open the target page and set filters.
2. Hover over the card → click the export or menu icon.
3. Select a format (CSV, Excel, or image).
4. **Expected result:** An export of the card data for the filters you selected.

## Share a page link

1. Copy the page URL from the browser.
2. Share only with people who should have access to REGIS FRANCHISEE APP.
3. Each recipient sees data according to their own account.

## Notes

- Export availability depends on your Domo role and card settings.
- Do not share exported salon data outside your organization without approval.

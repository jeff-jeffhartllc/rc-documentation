## Purpose

How to export data from REGIS FRANCHISEE APP pages. Exports contain only your PDP-scoped data.

## Export a card

1. Open the target page and set filters.
2. Hover over the card → click export/menu icon.
3. Select format (CSV, Excel, image).
4. **Expected result:** Export contains only your franchisee's salon data.

## Share a page link

1. Copy the page URL from the browser.
2. Share only with users who have REGIS FRANCHISEE APP access.
3. Each recipient sees only their own PDP-scoped data.

## Notes

- Franchisee users typically have **Participant** role — export availability depends on role and card settings.
- Do not share exported files containing salon data outside your organization without approval.

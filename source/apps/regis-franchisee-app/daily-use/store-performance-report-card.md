## Purpose

Monthly store-level report card with cumulative scores and same-store sales metrics, scoped to your franchisee's salons.

## Key cards

| Card | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| Report Card | Monthly store scores |
| Avg Cumulative Score — In Scope | Average score across your in-scope salons |
| SSS — MTD / QTD | Same-store sales metrics |

## Business purpose

REGIS FRANCHISEE APP gives franchisee users access to a **focused subset** of the main REGIS APP experience. It is designed for franchise operators who need visibility into their own stores without seeing corporate-wide or other franchisees' data.

## Relationship to REGIS APP

| Aspect | REGIS APP | REGIS FRANCHISEE APP |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Scope | Full corporate view | Franchisee subset |
| Audience | Corporate users | Franchisee users |
| Content | Complete app | Subset of main app pages/cards |
| Data access | Organization-wide (role-dependent) | Limited via **PDP** to a franchisee's stores |

**Personalized Data Permissions (PDP)** restrict what each franchisee user can see. Documentation for PDP configuration, testing, and troubleshooting belongs in both the franchisee app's maintenance docs and the shared cross-app reference.

See `shared/regis-app-relationship.pdf` for the full cross-app reference.

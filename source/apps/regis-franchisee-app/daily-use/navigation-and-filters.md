## Purpose

This guide explains how to navigate REGIS FRANCHISEE APP and use page filters within your PDP-assigned store scope.

## Opening the app

1. Sign in to https://regiscorp.domo.com.
2. Open **REGIS FRANCHISEE APP** or go directly to:  
   https://regiscorp.domo.com/app-studio/2028360971/pages/1195391822
3. **Expected result:** **Franchisee Performance** page loads with your stores' data.

## Page navigation

| Page | When to use |
| --- | --- |
| Franchisee Performance | Daily review of sales, traffic, productivity for your salons |
| Store Performance Report Card | Monthly store coaching scores |
| Store Performance Scorecard | SPH and sales trend analysis |
| Reference | Filter definitions and usage tips |

## Filters

Filters operate **within your PDP-assigned stores**. You cannot filter to salons outside your franchisee assignment.

| Filter | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Brand | Your brands (multi-brand franchisees see all their brands) |
| Salon | Only your assigned salons appear |
| DMA | Derived market area |
| From Date / Through Date | Date range |
| Country, State / Province, Territory | Geographic (within your scope) |
| Is Active? | Default: Active Only |
| Is PY Comp? | Default: PY Comp Only |
| Rollup Levels | Default: DMA |

**Not present:** Entity Type (PDP replaces this).

## Common tasks

### Task: View a single salon's performance

1. Open **Franchisee Performance**.
2. Select the **Salon** filter → choose the salon.
3. Review sales, traffic, and productivity cards.
4. **Expected result:** All cards show data for the selected salon only.

### Task: Compare salons within your franchisee

1. Leave **Salon** filter at "All" (or select multiple).
2. Review **AUV — Top 5 / Bottom 5** and **Top 5 Locations by Net Sales**.
3. **Expected result:** Rankings among your assigned salons only.

## If this fails

| Symptom | Action |
| --- | --- |
| No data on any page | Contact support — PDP assignment may be missing |
| Salon missing from filter | Contact support — salon may not be assigned to your franchisee |
| See another franchisee's data | **Escalate immediately** — PDP misconfiguration |

## Purpose

How to open REGIS FRANCHISEE APP, move between pages, and use filters for your salons.

## Opening the app

1. Sign in to https://regiscorp.domo.com.
2. Open **REGIS FRANCHISEE APP** or go directly to:  
   https://regiscorp.domo.com/app-studio/2028360971/pages/1195391822
3. **Expected result:** **Franchisee Performance** loads with your stores' data.

## Page navigation

| Page | When to use |
| --- | --- |
| Franchisee Performance | Daily review of sales, traffic, and productivity |
| Store Performance Report Card | Monthly store coaching scores |
| Store Performance Scorecard | SPH and sales trend analysis |
| Reference | Filter definitions and usage tips |

## Filters

| Filter | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Brand | Brands available on your account |
| Salon | Salons available on your account |
| DMA | Derived market area |
| From Date / Through Date | Date range |
| Country, State / Province, Territory | Geographic filters |
| Is Active? | Default: Active Only |
| Is PY Comp? | Default: PY Comp Only |
| Rollup Levels | Default: DMA |

## Common tasks

### Task: View a single salon's performance

1. Open **Franchisee Performance**.
2. Open the **Salon** filter and choose the salon.
3. Review sales, traffic, and productivity cards.
4. **Expected result:** Cards show data for the selected salon only.

### Task: Compare your salons

1. Leave **Salon** at All (or select more than one).
2. Review rankings such as top / bottom locations by sales.
3. **Expected result:** Rankings among salons on your account.

## If this fails

| Symptom | Action |
| --- | --- |
| No data on any page | Contact your Domo administrator or franchisee support |
| Salon missing from the filter | Contact your administrator to confirm salon assignments |
| Unexpected salons appear | Contact your administrator immediately |
